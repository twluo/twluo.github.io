Music Transcriber

This utility helps translate from Alphabetical Musical Notation to something you can paste on Xerca's Music Maker Mod

Each note is divided into 3 parts: The note (ranging from C to B), if it's a sharp(#), flat(b) or no augmentation(_), and the octave (1-4). Ex: C_3 denotes C natural on the 3rd octave.
Additionally silences are denoted as 000.
